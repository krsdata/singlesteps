Start Up Consultancy


At Single Step Solutions, we work with early stage, expansion and high growth opportunities companies. We offer unique expertise that blends successful entrepreneurial experience, academic or legal insight and practical business analysis.

We specialize in bringing clarity to complex business models and in creating plans tailored for a sophisticated investor audience. We ensure that all written materials viz. business plan, financial models, executive summary and pitch deck are up to VC standards.

As Start-up experts, we help companies right from formulating the mission & vision statement, to devising business strategies & plans and assist in executing the same. We provide end to end service to our clients whether it is managing finance or accounting or human resource management.
Our spectrum of service includes:
  • Business ideation preparation & analysis
  • Financial Modeling
  • Assistance in building the team and management
  • Preparation of Pitch Deck & detailed Project Report
  • Devising strategy roadmap for the new venture
  • Undertaking CMA Data preparation
  • Investor Sourcing & Outreach program
  • Negotiations with VC/ Private Equity Firms
  • Setting up business & information system
