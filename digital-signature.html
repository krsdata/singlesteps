Digital Signature


We provide DSC that can integrate with applications such as emails, workflow, enterprise wide applications, or secure VPNs.

Our endeavour is to empower organizations with IT solutions to improve business efficiencies and make transactions in e-Governance, e-Commerce, e-Management, safe, secure and non-repudiable.
Brief Overview of DSC
A digital signature is an electronic signature that can be used to authenticate the identity of the sender of a message or the signer of a document, and to ensure that the original content of the message or document that has been sent is unchanged. Thus Digital Signatures provide the following three features:-
  • Authentication
  • Integrity
  • Non-repudiation
Why DSC?
A Digital Signature Certificate authenticates your identity electronically. DSC also provides you with a high level of security for your online transactions by ensuring absolute privacy of the information exchanged using a DSC. It is pertinent to note that, DSC can be used to sign / encrypt information such that only the intended recipient can read it. Further, you can digitally sign information to assure the recipient that it has not been changed in transit, and also verify your identity as the sender of the message.
Use of DSC
  • For sending and receiving digitally signed and encrypted emails/ documents.
  • For carrying out secure web-based transactions.
  • In e-tendering, e-procurement for Registrar of Companies e-filing, Income Tax for e-filing income tax returns and also in many other applications.
  • For signing documents like MS Word, MS Excel and PDFs.
  • Plays a pivotal role in creating a paperless office.
