about us

Single Step Solutions is a firm based in Indore (M.P.) which provides various business and management related services under one roof. Our endeavor is to provide quality services and ensure that the services we deliver are aimed at supporting organizations in running efficiently and effectively, with strong accountability and governance. Stemming from your needs, we provide expert services that address all aspects relevant to a business right from conceptualisation to implementation and continuance.



Vision :


Our vision is to cater every business solution at just one place and be the well-known pioneer across the globe.

Our clients value our collaborative approach. Right from inception, our founders have emphasised the importance of professional standards and personalised service; and to this day, we continue to reflect this progressive mindset by serving our clients with integrity, delivering high quality, innovative results. Our ability to identify, understand and resolve a variety of issues and concerns for our clients comes from our years of experience across a wide range of industries and geographies, combined with a versatile group of professionals and a multi-disciplinary team.


Our spectrum of service offerings include :


  • Manpower Recruitment Service
  • Payroll Management
  • Financial Services
  • Tax Advisory & Compliance Services
  • Corporate Advisory Services
  • Start-up Consultancy
  • Business Management Consultancy
  • Issuance of Digital Signature


Our customer focused approach, commitment to timelines and the quality of our processes has resulted in a very large and loyal client base comprising of Multinational Companies, leading Indian Business Houses as well as several Small & Medium Enterprises. We serve clients across the globe.
